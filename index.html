Visual Analytics for Better Decisions

I’m Chihua Ma (马驰华), Director of Decision Sciences Visual Analytics at Epsilon. I lead a team of data scientists building visual analytics systems that turn complex data into clear stories and actionable decisions.

I have 8+ years of AdTech experience across data visualization, human-computer interaction, and AI/ML. I hold a PhD in Computer Science from UIC (2018) and I’m currently pursuing an MBA at Northwestern Kellogg.
